About me



My name is Ana Margarida Costa and I'm from Caldas da Rainha, Portugal.

I have a degree in Human Relations and Organizational Comunication. I've worked in administration for a time, but I always complemented it with Marketing, Online Content Management and Accounting. In one of my job experiences, I also did Housing Tourism.
